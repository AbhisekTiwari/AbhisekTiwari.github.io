About Me

Hi, I am a research scholar (PMRF) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Patna, India. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Sriparna Saha and Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya. I am a part of the AI-ML-NLP group at Indian Institute of Technology, Patna. Before joining IIT Patna, I completed B.tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Government College of Engineering and Textile Technology, Berhampore in 2019.

I broadly work in the area of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Artificial Intelligence and Reinforcement Learning. More specifically, my curent research focuses on Dialogue System, Clinical NLP and AI for Social Good. I consider myself a dedicated researcher and innovator, driven to delve into critical aspects of AI and contribute to challenging real-world problems by translating the findings/insights into practical innovations.
