About Me

Hi, I am a research scholar (PMRF) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Patna, India. I am a part of the AI-ML-NLP group at IIT Patna. My PhD. advisors are Dr. Sriparna Saha and Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya. Before joining IIT Patna, I completed B.tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Government College of Engineering and Textile Technology, Berhampore in 2019.

I broadly work in the area of Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Reinforcement Learning. More specifically, my curent research focuses on Dialogue System, AI for Social Good and Clinical NLP.
