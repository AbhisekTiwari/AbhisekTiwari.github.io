About Me

Hello, I am a passionate researcher and innovator, committed to exploring critical aspects of AI to address pressing real-world problems. I broadly work in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Healthcare, and Reinforcement Learning. More specifically, my current work focuses on AI for Healthcare, Sample Efficient Learning, Clinical Decision Support (CDS), and AI for Social Good.

I completed my PhD in computer science and engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Patna, specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Artificial Intelligence, and Reinforcement Learning. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Sriparna Saha and Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya.I was a recipient of the prestigious Prime Minister's Research Fellowship (PMRF) award for my doctoral study and have made a significant contribution to the medical dialogue system. The findings are reported through patents and publications in esteemed journals and conferences such as IEEE Transactions on AI, KBS, ACL, ACM Multimedia, CIKM, Coling, ECAI, and ECIR. Beyond this, our works have also garnered various recognitions in commercial space, such as national media coverage, Microsoft-supported startup recognition, a bronze medal in Inter IIT 2023, and a nomination for GPAI 2023.
